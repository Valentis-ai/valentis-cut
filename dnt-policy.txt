# Policy
VALENTIS Cut ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy and honors the 
Do Not Track (DNT) preference signal sent by your browser.

# What is DNT?
Do Not Track is a technology and policy proposal that enables users to 
opt out of tracking by websites they do not visit, including analytics 
services, advertising networks, and social platforms.

# Our Commitment
When we detect a DNT signal from your browser:
1. We do not track your browsing activity across third-party websites
2. We disable non-essential analytics cookies
3. We do not serve personalized advertisements
4. We limit data collection to essential service functionality only

# Exceptions
DNT signals do not affect:
- Essential cookies required for site functionality
- Security and fraud prevention measures
- Aggregate, non-identifiable usage statistics
- Data required for video processing and editing services
